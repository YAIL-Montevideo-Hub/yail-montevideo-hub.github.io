AI for Good – Montevideo Hub

Young AI Leaders Uruguay 🇺🇾

🌍 About Us

We are the only Uruguayan hub of the global AI for Good initiative. Our mission is to empower young people to use AI to generate social, environmental, and cultural impact through sustainable tech solutions.

🎯 Our Goals

  • Promote social well-being and equity
  • Protect the environment and the planet
  • Foster prosperity and innovation

🙋 Who Can Join?

  • People aged 18–30
  • Located in Montevideo or nearby
  • No coding experience required — just motivation!

🚀 What We Do

  • AI software projects for social good
  • Community outreach & events
  • Dataset creation & model training
  • Use of LLMs and modern AI tools
